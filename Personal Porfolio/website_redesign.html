Skills Used and Why

My group all had to use various skills to complete this project. It took knowledge of things like wireframing, coding, and analytics to do well. When writing out the creative brief we had to research J.Gilligans to see their location, what kind of clientele they had. The price range of the clientele. Peak hours of operation and more. When wireframing our site we needed knowledge of wirframing programs such as XD to create mockups for our new site. We also created components using various principles that we learned in CTEC 3350. We did this to see what some optimal layouts were and how we would go about creating them later in our coding software.
