J.Gilligans Bar & Grill:Shuttle Service

We are located on 400 East Abram St in Arlington, Texas. To contact us please call 817-274-8561. To take the shuttle to the games just wait outside. The NFL will limit bags that are allowed into AT&T Stadium. In order to comply with this we have to enforce some restrictions on bags. Bags that are not allowed include backpacks, purses, camera cases, diaper bags, binocular cases, fanny packs, printed pattern platic bags, and seat cushions. Items that are allowed include bags that are clear platic, vinyl, or pvc and do not exceed 12 inches. One gallon clear plastic freezer bags. Small clutch bags approximately the size of a hand with or without a handle strap. Binoculars or a phone or camera as long as they are not in their own bag. Diapers can be carried in a clear plastic bag.
